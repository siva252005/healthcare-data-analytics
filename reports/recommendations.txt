📌 RECOMMENDATIONS – HEALTHCARE ANALYSIS

1. Implement Smoking Reduction Programs:
Since smokers incur significantly higher healthcare costs (~280% more), insurance companies and healthcare providers should promote anti-smoking campaigns and wellness programs.

2. Encourage Healthy BMI:
Introduce fitness programs, diet plans, and awareness campaigns to reduce obesity levels, as high BMI is strongly linked to increased medical expenses.

3. Focus on High-Risk Patients:
Closely monitor and provide preventive care for high-risk individuals (smoker + high BMI + age > 50) to reduce future healthcare costs.

4. Region-Based Strategy:
The Southeast region shows the highest healthcare costs. Targeted interventions such as lifestyle awareness programs and better healthcare access should be implemented.

5. Preventive Healthcare Investment:
Encourage regular health checkups and early diagnosis to reduce long-term treatment costs.

6. Personalized Insurance Plans:
Design dynamic premium models based on risk categories (Low, Medium, High Risk) for better pricing and customer segmentation.

7. Awareness Campaigns:
Educate people about the impact of smoking, obesity, and aging on healthcare costs to promote healthier lifestyles.
